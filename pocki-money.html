Pocki Money

The challenge

The goal was to design an easy to use budgeting tool that can be used across multiple devices and is tailored to students and their financial situation. I wanted the branding and aesthetic to be fun and not typical of the sector to help make a potentially tedious task of keeping on top of your finances into something effortless and engaging.

Branding

The brand was more in line with other services aimed at students rather than other financial or banking services. I designed brand assets and put together guidelines to ensure the tool had a cohesive and distinctive style.
