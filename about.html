About

My Story

Hi, I’m Charlie Rose. I’m a UI designer, which is fortunate because I struggle to go a day without creating something!

I’m fascinated by how the human mind works, which is why before teaching myself design I originally studied cognitive neuroscience, a great foundation for any creative pursuit let alone for building user-interfaces. However, I couldn’t ignore my fundamental need to create. I get a real kick out of problem solving, particularly using a combination of psychology, design and creativity to find solutions.

I’m particularly excited by the future of human-computer interaction and how this will develop as we further explore the possibilities of virtual reality. Very exciting times!
